Projects

Desert Island 3D model (Jan, 2023)

Blender project

Creating a Desert Island 3D model in anticipation of summer days and beach season was a fun and engaging way to explore the creativity and imagination of 3D modeling in Blender. The project allowed me experiment with various techniques in Blender: starting form efficient mirror modification technique to applying realistic materials to 3D objects. I believe that with this project, I demonstrated texture mapping, lighting, and object placement, while also providing a space for artistic expression through the design and creation of a visually stunning scene.

Fashion Recommender System (September, 2022)

Python (Pandas, NumPy,Matplotlib, Seaborn, Flask, Keras, Scikit-learn, TF)

Developing a fashion recommendation system using the nearest neighbor algorithm and image feature vectors extracted by ResNET50 architecture was a challenging and rewarding project. Through this project, I gained a deep understanding of how recommendation systems work and the importance of user interaction data in improving their accuracy. I also learned how to use advanced deep learning techniques, such as feature extraction with ResNET50 architecture, to extract meaningful features from image data.

"Tazala"/"CleanUP" UX/UI Case Study and Website (Aug-Sept, 2021)

A case study with Figma/ Website (HTML,CSS)

TAZALA is a volunteering project that focuses on organizing and executing community clean-up events in various neighborhoods. The project aims to promote environmental awareness and encourage people to take responsibility for their surroundings. Volunteers from all walks of life come together to pick up litter, remove debris, and tidy up public spaces, such as parks, beaches, and streets. By participating in TAZALA, volunteers can contribute to their communities, learn about environmental issues, and build meaningful connections with others who share their values. The project's impact can be seen in the tangible results of cleaner and healthier public spaces, as well as the intangible benefits of increased community engagement and pride.
